ARTIST

Jason deCaires Taylor

A world-renowned British environmental sculptor, and a pioneer in promoting the recovery of marine ecosystems through art by installing sculptures underwater.

The sculpture sunk off Tokunoshima is Jason's first environmentally regenerative underwater sculpture project in Japan. Produced by RATA, it launched as a new form of regenerative art tourism connecting Tokunoshima's nature, culture, and tourism.

Project Timeline

  • Jun 2023RATA begins developing the concept with Jason
  • Feb 2024On-site research and concept gathering on Tokunoshima
  • Oct 2024Production begins, with a pregnant figure as the motif
  • Oct 2025Sunk off the Inokawa coast as Japan's first environmentally regenerative underwater sculpture

Actress Kiko Mizuhara, who cherishes the sea of Tokunoshima, took part as the sculpture's model and inspiration. (Source: Tokunoshima Town)
