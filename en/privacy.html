Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

The Regenerative Art Tourism Association ("RATA") handles personal information collected through pre-registration on this website ("the Site") in accordance with the following policy.

1. Information We Collect

Through the pre-registration form, we collect the following information.

  • Email address (required)
  • Name (optional)
  • Country/region of residence, preferred language
  • Intent to visit Tokunoshima, areas of interest, expected price, number of companions, reason for interest (including optional fields)

2. Purpose of Use

  • Sending updates and notices about the RATA Pass
  • Statistical analysis for demand validation and service planning (aggregated, non-identifying)
  • Responding to inquiries from registrants

3. Disclosure to Third Parties

Except as required by law, we will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your consent.

4. Service Providers

We may use external services (such as hosting and database providers) to operate the Site, send emails, and store data. We require appropriate data handling from any such providers.

5. Retention Period

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes above, and will promptly act on any deletion request from a registrant.

6. Requests for Disclosure or Deletion

If you would like to view, correct, or delete your registered information, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact

Regenerative Art Tourism Association
Email: info@rata.jp

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be revised as necessary. Any revised version takes effect once posted on the Site.
